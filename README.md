# JOBTELLECT - Job Market Intelligence Dashboard

## Overview

JOBTELLECT is a labor market intelligence platform that analyzes **123K+ job postings** and **213K+ skill mappings** to uncover hiring trends, in-demand skills, salary patterns, and career insights.

The project combines **SQL, Python, MySQL, and Power BI** to transform raw employment data into actionable intelligence through interactive dashboards and business-focused analytics.

---

## Problem Statement

Job seekers often struggle to understand:

* Which skills are currently in demand
* What salary ranges can be expected
* Which locations offer the best opportunities
* How hiring demand varies across industries and experience levels

JOBTELLECT addresses these challenges by consolidating job market data into a comprehensive analytics solution that supports data-driven career decisions.

---

## Project Objectives

* Analyze overall hiring trends and job market activity
* Identify the most in-demand skills
* Explore salary patterns across locations and experience levels
* Examine geographic distribution of opportunities
* Generate actionable career insights using data analytics

## Dashboard Modules

### Market Overview

Provides a high-level summary of hiring activity, work-type distribution, experience demand, and top skills.

### Skills Intelligence

Analyzes skill demand, category trends, and skill distribution across job opportunities.

### Salary Intelligence

Explores salary progression, compensation patterns, top-paying companies, and location-based salary insights.

### Location Intelligence

Examines geographic hiring demand, regional job distribution, and opportunity concentration.

---

## Key Insights

* Full-Time positions dominate overall hiring demand.
* Mid-Senior and Entry-Level roles account for a significant share of opportunities.
* Technology and analytical skills remain highly sought after.
* Executive and Director positions command the highest salaries.
* Major metropolitan regions demonstrate stronger hiring activity and compensation potential.
